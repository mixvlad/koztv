I really did a lot.

2026-01-19 · View in

Projects, technologies, attempts to optimize everything, understand faster, decide more accurately. Over time, I started noticing, that perhaps the point isn't in the amount of work done, but in the quality of the choices. This channel is an attempt to figure out how we think and choose in a world that is constantly accelerating. Sometimes through technology. Sometimes through chess. More often — through the pause between decisions. Without haste, without fuss.
